# Systemic Foundation
## Conceptual Technical Reference of Aptadynamic Cybernetics

The **Systemic Foundation** is the discipline's conceptual technical reference: the published work that fixes the vocabulary, the principles, and the problem the discipline studies — the distinction between a system that sustains itself and one that merely appears to.

Its role within the discipline:

- It is the **source of the framework's concepts**: the constitutive trigon (opening, sustainment, pruning), admissible variability, structural cost, latent collapse as the imitation of coherence, persistence as distinct from mere duration.
- It is a **reference, not a dependency**: none of it is required to run, implement, or extend the PRAMA Protokol. The normative path for implementers is the [Aptadynamic Cybernetics Specification (AS-1)](../specification/AS-1.md); the formal path for theorists is the [Logical–Mathematical Corpus](../corpus/README.md).
- Its terminology enters the technical language exclusively through the [Translation Glossary](../glossary/translation-glossary.md), which is the official bridge.

The Systemic Foundation precedes every domain category: the same viability regime it describes is recognizable in a power grid, an institution, a market, or an organism. That anteriority is what the Protokol's kernel/observation separation (AS-1, P7) translates into architecture.
