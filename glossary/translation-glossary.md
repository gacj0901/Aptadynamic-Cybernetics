# Translation Glossary
## Systemic Foundation → Technical Terminology of Cybernetics

**Status:** In preparation.

This document is the official bridge between the terminology of the **Systemic Foundation** (the discipline's conceptual technical reference) and the technical language of Aptadynamic Cybernetics.

It is organized in six families:

A. Substrate and regimes
B. The constitutive trigon
C. Dynamic magnitudes
D. States and transitions
E. Epistemic level (the self-observing system)
F. Protocol architecture

Each entry maps: *Systemic Foundation term → technical term (EN) → formal symbol → locus (Corpus / Specification / Engine).*

The canonical technical terms fixed by this glossary are the ones used in the [Aptadynamic Cybernetics Specification (AS-1)](../specification/AS-1.md).
